About Skills Development Hub

SD HUB was established and managed by PSF and sponsored by SEED & Helping Hand Foundation. The idea of this skills development hub stemmed from a collective desire to bridge the gap between job seekers and industry requirements and make trainees job-ready. As a result, we have dedicated ourselves to providing high-quality, job-oriented training programs free of cost.
